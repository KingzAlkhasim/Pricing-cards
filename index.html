FAQ

Good to know.

How fast are alerts actually sent?

Most incidents are detected and paged in under 30 seconds from the failed check, using the region closest to your service.

Can I move from another monitoring tool?

Yes — import your monitors via CSV or our API, and our team will help you migrate on-call schedules for free.

Do you offer a self-hosted option?

Enterprise plans include a self-hosted or single-tenant deployment for teams with strict data residency needs.

What happens after the trial ends?

You'll be prompted to pick a plan. Nothing is charged automatically, and your monitors are paused, not deleted.
